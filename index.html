About Me

Robotics engineer specialized in space technologies and aerial systems, I currently design, prototype, and field-test multimodal drones for extreme environments.

After completing my Master's thesis research at Caltech on reinforcement-learning navigation, I graduated from EPFL in 2024 with an M.Sc. in Robotics and Space Technologies.

During my studies, I joined the EPFL Rocket Team as Avionics Engineer and later co-founded and led EPFL Xplore, a 100+ members initiative whose planetary rovers secured three consecutive podiums at the European Rover Challenge from 2021 to 2023, and earned Excellence Awards in Autonomous Navigation, Autonomous Manipulation, Science and Probing.
